Pre-Sales Call FAQ

These are the most common questions busy guys ask before they start coaching. Read through them so you show up to the call clear and ready.

How is this different from what I’ve already tried?

You have probably tried generic programs, PDF plans, or YouTube routines that treat everyone the same. They are built for “average people” and assume you have endless time and energy.

This coaching is different because everything is built around you: your job hours, stress levels, sleep, training history, food preferences, and current energy. We use your data each week to adjust.

No more guessing. No more starting again on Monday.

Do I need access to a gym or special equipment?

No. If you have a commercial gym, great. If you only have dumbbells, bands, or bodyweight at home, we build around that.

Many busy clients train in small windows of time at home or in a basic gym and still see serious results. The structure matters more than the equipment.

What if I’m too busy to follow the program?

Most clients are exactly where you are: full-time work, long days, mental fatigue, often a partner and social life on top.

The entire system is designed for busy men. Sessions are efficient, focused, and realistic. Nutrition is simplified and repeatable. You are not asked to train 6 days a week or meal prep for hours.

The goal is to save you time and energy, not drain more of it.

What if I’ve tried other coaches and it did not work?

That is a common story. Many coaches hand you a recycled plan and disappear.

Here, the coaching is built on:

  • Deep individualisation to your life and body
  • Data and feedback every week
  • Clear communication and honest expectations

If you are coachable and follow the system, you will progress. We focus on long-term, sustainable change, not short-term hype.

Will I be hungry all the time or feel restricted?

No. You will be eating real food in real portions that suit your body and your lifestyle. No crash dieting, no tiny meals, no weird rules.

Most busy guys are surprised at how much they can eat when the structure is right. The goal is controlled, sustainable progress, not suffering.

What happens if I fall off track?

Life happens. Work blows up, kids get sick, travel comes up. You will never be left on your own to “figure it out.”

When things slip, we look at the week, identify what went wrong, adjust your plan, and move forward. No guilt. No lectures. Only strategy.

Consistency beats perfection. You do not fail because you had one bad week.

I am not sure I can afford it right now…

Fair question. The real question is: can you afford to stay where you are?

Think about what low energy, low confidence, and poor health are costing you right now — in your work, relationships, and day-to-day life.

Coaching is not a random expense. It is an investment into a stronger, sharper version of you that shows up better in every area.

Is there a guarantee?

Yes. If you show up, follow the plan, communicate honestly, and stay coachable, and you do not get results, we will coach you for free until you do.

The real risk is doing nothing and staying stuck for another year.

What kind of results have your clients seen?

Busy men have:

  • Dropped 10–20 kg of body fat
  • Built lean muscle while working full-time
  • Fixed low energy and afternoon crashes
  • Regained confidence in and out of the gym

You are not an exception. If they can do it with work and life commitments, so can you.

How do I know if I am the right fit?

This is for you if:

  • You are busy, but ready to take ownership
  • You are tired of guesswork and random programs
  • You want simple structure you can actually follow

This is not for you if you want a magic pill, shortcuts, or someone to blame when it gets hard.

Before Our Call: Be Honest With Yourself

Take 60 seconds to read these. If most of your answers are “no”, coaching is probably the right move.

  • Do you feel fully in control of your health, strength, and energy levels each week?
  • Do you consistently follow a training and nutrition plan built specifically for your body and goals?
  • Have you created the discipline to train without skipping or second-guessing yourself?
  • Are you showing up every day as the strongest, most confident version of yourself?
  • Do you feel proud of the way your body looks and performs right now?
  • Do you wake up feeling rested and energised, not sluggish and drained?
  • Do you feel confident taking your shirt off or seeing yourself in the mirror?
  • Are your workouts actually moving you closer to the body and performance you want?
  • Have you already achieved the transformation you promised yourself this year?
  • Are you seeing steady, measurable progress every week in strength, physique, or energy?
  • Do you know exactly what your body needs right now — and are you giving it that consistently?
  • Do you believe your current routine is worth the time and effort you are putting into it?
  • If nothing changes, are you comfortable being in the same shape and energy level a year from now?

If the honest answer to most of these is “no”, that is exactly what we fix together.
